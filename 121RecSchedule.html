Recitation Schedule: Monday through Friday

For CS 120/121 there is a recitation each week for your lecture. In recitation there are quizzes that account for 5% of your grade. These quizzes cannot be made up if you miss recitation. That being said, you can go to another recitation and take the quiz. The submission process will automatically put your grade into the correct course. If you cannot make another recitation do not worry there is flexibility in this system. To get all 5% of the recitation quiz grade you need to complete any 5 quizzes, but at least 7 quizzes will be given over the semester.
