Guidance for giving presentation

Saving the Presentation

Each classroom's technology varies and downloading your presentation from the cloud can cause formatting issues. It is suggested that you bring your presentation as a .pptx and .pdf on a USB memory stick.

Time

There are many groups and not much time. That being said ensure that all group members arrive early and everything is ready before class starts. The presentation cannot be delayed because a member is running late.
